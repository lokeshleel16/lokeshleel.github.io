<aside>
💡 We see that our generation is kind of lost in finding the purpose of life and has many times failed to live life to its simplest. I feel that one of the main reasons is the disconnection between the children and their grandparents.

In order to solve this problem at some scale I'm thinking of creating a platform that will connect teenagers with people experienced in life. Connecting the new generation to the old.

The flow goes roughly like this. Children from the age group 13-25 years and similarly aged people from 50 years onwards will register on the platform. We will connect them based on interest for a one-on-one session with the opportunity for the newer generation to listen to experiences from school/college, world tour and stories about human connection, cultural diversity, stories about success and failure from veterans of life. Whereas the older generation gets the opportunity to share and simultaneously learn about situations that younger generation is going through in their free time.

</aside>
